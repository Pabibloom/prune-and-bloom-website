Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Prune and Bloom's garden services

How often do you recommend garden maintenance visits?

The frequency of maintenance visits depends on your garden's size, complexity, and how quickly it grows. For most gardens, a visit every 2-4 weeks during the growing season (March-October) and monthly visits during winter is ideal. I'll assess your garden's specific needs and recommend a schedule that keeps it looking its best year-round.

What happens during an initial consultation?

During our first meeting, we'll walk through your garden together while I listen to your vision, concerns, and requirements. I'll assess the site conditions, existing plants, and potential opportunities for enhancement. Afterward, I'll provide recommendations tailored to your garden's needs and your personal preferences. This consultation typically takes 60-90 minutes.

Do you provide all plants for enhancement and design projects?

Yes, I source all plants for enhancement and design projects, selecting quality specimens that are well-suited to your garden's conditions. I have relationships with excellent local and specialist nurseries to ensure the best selection. If you have specific plants you'd like included, I'm happy to incorporate them into the plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, cash, and checks. For ongoing maintenance services, I typically invoice monthly. For design and enhancement projects, I request a deposit before work begins, with the balance due upon completion.

Are you insured?

Yes, I carry comprehensive public liability insurance and professional indemnity insurance, providing peace of mind for both you and me.
