Services

Expert Garden Maintenance

  • Pruning and plant care - Pruning to enhance plant health and appearance
  • Seasonal adjustments and preparations - Ensuring your garden looks its best throughout the year
  • Lawn and border management - Maintaining crisp edges and healthy growth
  • Plant health assessments - Identifying issues before they become problems

Each visit leaves your garden looking its best, with every plant performing to its potential.

Refresh & Enhancement

For gardens that need tweaking without a full redesign. Garden refresh focuses on strategic improvements to elevate your existing outdoor space.

  • Strategic plant additions and replacements - Introducing key specimens to transform the garden's appearance
  • Creating focal points - Establishing visual anchors that draw the eye and create structure
  • Seasonal colour planning - Ensuring visual interest throughout the year
  • Pots and planters - Adding movable accents that can transform spaces instantly
  • Small-scale renovations - Targeted improvements to existing features and planted areas

Garden refresh can breathe new life into tired spaces, creating renewed enjoyment and appreciation of your outdoor area.

Design Services

  • Full garden redesign - Comprehensive transformation of your outdoor space
  • Planting plans - Detailed specifications tailored to your site conditions
  • Plant sourcing and installation - Quality specimens selected and properly planted
  • Specialized garden features - From kitchen gardens to wildlife havens
  • Garden assessment and planning - Expert guidance to shape your garden vision

My design services combine my horticultural experience with fresh creative ideas to produce gardens that enhance your lifestyle and property.
